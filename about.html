About Us

We at,Entrepreneurship development cell started with a great vision of shaping the future Entrepreneurs & to transform the world for a better living place with great discipline , entrepreneurial culture and moreover with great ethics…

ED-CELL works with a dedicated team along with actively working faculty . CELL also supports all the aspirants with mentoring , planning, monitoring and help to execute their ideas into startup’s and into a real business .

And our mission is to create successful startup’s that are helpful for the society and being a part of making INDIA as one of the leading countries in Entrepreneurship .


What We Do...

Empathyze..

As part of our core values and mission we organize different activities and events to promote and motivate students among Entrepreneurship through conducting webinars from successful Entrepreneurs , E-Summits , workshops of both technical as well as entrepreneurial oriented.

We organise events in the campus for the students to share their ideas and accelerate their work with the help of Management.


Innovate..



We ignites the passion and spirit among the students to achieve their goals and to make them useful for the Society.

We are intended to develop and strengthen Entrepreneurial qualities in the budding professions who are interested in starting their own ventures .

Independible..



We uplift the students by creating the environment of self employment.

We strive to make the budding entrepreneurs imbibed with leadership qualities using innovative and ethical businesses pratices to make global impact.
